About Me

Who am i?

I am a Toronto-based music producer & sound engineer. My love for music, beat-making, and engineering started as a young kid in my parents' basement. My unique skill and technique were developed while learning from industry professionals at Metal Works Institute and my knowledge and talent continue to grow through hands-on experience doing independent and ad hoc projects for well-known brands and artists.
