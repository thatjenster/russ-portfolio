About Me

Russ Perna is a Toronto-based freelance Music Producer and self-trained Audio/Sound Engineer. Growing up in Richmond Hill, Russ had an undeniable love for the art of music and started learning industry level digital audio workstation programs and software such as FL Studio and ProTools at an early age. He furthered his music production and sound engineering education at Canada’s Premier Entertainment Arts School, Metal Works Institute then continued to build on his skillset independently. Today, Russ has worked with well-known companies and brands such as Maple Leafs Sports & Entertainment (MLSE), Live Nation, American Express, and Mike’s Hard Lemonade. Aside from producing hundreds of beats for personal projects, Russ has mixed and mastered audio and sound for recognizable music artists, comedians, and TV personalities and his works have been posted online as well as broadcasted with Rogers for major TV networks such as CityTV.
